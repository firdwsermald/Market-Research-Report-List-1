<p><p>The junction box market consists of two primary types: non-metallic and metallic junction boxes. Non-metallic junction boxes are typically made from materials like PVC or fiberglass, offering corrosion resistance and lightweight characteristics, making them ideal for indoor applications. On the other hand, metallic junction boxes, typically crafted from steel or aluminum, provide enhanced durability and strength, suitable for outdoor or industrial environments where robustness is crucial. Both types serve essential roles in electrical installations by protecting wiring and connections.</p></p>
