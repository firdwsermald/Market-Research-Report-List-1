<p><p>The sanitary plug valves market offers two main types: sanitary 2-way and sanitary 3-way plug valves. Sanitary 2-way plug valves are designed to control the flow of liquids in a single direction, ensuring efficient flow regulation in hygienic applications. In contrast, sanitary 3-way plug valves facilitate flow diversion and mixing, allowing for versatile routing of liquids in various processes. Both types are essential in industries like food, beverage, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, where cleanliness and integrity of the fluid are critical.</p></p>
