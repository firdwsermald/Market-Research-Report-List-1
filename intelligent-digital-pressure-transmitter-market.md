<p><strong>The Intelligent Digital Pressure Transmitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>'Absolute Pressure Transmitters'</li><li>'Differential Pressure Transmitters'</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent Digital Pressure Transmitter market includes two main types: Absolute Pressure Transmitters and Differential Pressure Transmitters. Absolute Pressure Transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, making them suitable for applications requiring precise pressure readings, such as in environments with varying atmospheric pressure. In contrast, Differential Pressure Transmitters measure the difference between two pressure points, commonly used in flow measurement and level detection. Both types incorporate advanced digital technologies for enhanced accuracy, reliability, and communication capabilities in industrial applications.</p></p>
