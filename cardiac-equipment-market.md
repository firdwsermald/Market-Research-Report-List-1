<p><p>The cardiac equipment market includes various devices essential for diagnosing and managing heart conditions. Balloon pumps support circulation during recovery or surgery, while cardiopulmonary bypass pumps facilitate blood circulation during cardiac procedures. Cardiac ultrasound devices provide imaging for diagnosis. CRT pacemakers (CRT-P) and CRT defibrillators (CRT-D) are used for heart rhythm management. Defibrillators restore normal heart rhythm in emergencies, while traditional pacemakers regulate heartbeats. Ventricular assist devices support heart function in advanced heart failure cases, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
