<p><p>The PVC heat shrink tube market is categorized into three main types based on wall thickness. Thin-walled PVC heat shrink tubes are lightweight and ideal for insulation and bundling in low-stress applications. Medium-walled tubes offer enhanced durability and provide better protection, making them suitable for a wider range of electrical and mechanical applications. Heavy-walled PVC heat shrink tubes are robust and designed for high-stress environments, offering superior insulation and mechanical support, often used in aerospace and industrial sectors.</p></p>
