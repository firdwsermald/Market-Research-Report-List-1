<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Electrocatalysts</li><li>Heterogeneous Electrocatalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrocatalyst market is divided into homogeneous and heterogeneous electrocatalysts. Homogeneous electrocatalysts operate in a single phase, typically in liquid solutions, offering uniform catalytic activity and better accessibility for reactants. Heterogeneous electrocatalysts, on the other hand, exist at the interface of different phases, such as solid catalysts in gaseous or liquid reactions, allowing for easier separation and reuse. Each type serves specific applications in energy conversion and storage, impacting efficiency and commercialization in various industries.</p></p>
