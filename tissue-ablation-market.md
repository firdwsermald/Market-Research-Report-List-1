<p><p>The tissue ablation market is diverse, featuring various technologies for targeted therapy. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat to destroy tissue, while ultrasound ablation utilizes sound waves for similar effects. Laser-based ablation employs focused light energy to cut or vaporize tissue. Irreversible electroporation disrupts cell membranes with electrical pulses. Cryoablation uses extreme cold, and microwave ablation generates heat through microwave energy. Hydrothermal ablation involves heated fluids, whereas external beam radiation therapy (EBRT) targets cancerous tissues from outside the body. Each method has distinct applications and benefits.</p></p>
