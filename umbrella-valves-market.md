<p><p>Umbrella valves market types include Long Tail Pull and Short Tail Push, which refer to different mechanisms for controlling fluid flow. Long Tail Pull valves operate by pulling a flexible membrane or flap, allowing fluid to flow in a specific direction while preventing backflow. Short Tail Push valves, on the other hand, utilize a push mechanism where the valve opens with pressure, allowing fluid to pass through. Both designs are essential in various applications, ensuring efficient fluid management in systems.</p></p>
