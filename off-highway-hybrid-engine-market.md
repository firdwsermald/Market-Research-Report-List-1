<p><p>The Off Highway Hybrid Engine Market is categorized into two main types: Plug-in Type and Non-plug-in Type. Plug-in Type hybrid engines can be charged from an external power source, allowing for extended electric-only operation and reduced fuel consumption. In contrast, Non-plug-in Type hybrid engines rely on internal combustion engines to recharge the battery while operating, offering improved fuel efficiency without the need for external charging. Both types aim to enhance performance while reducing emissions in off-highway applications.</p></p>
