<p><p>The valve for defense market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions. Globe valves are designed for regulating flow, providing enhanced control over fluid movement. Gate valves allow for full flow when fully open and are used primarily for isolation purposes. Other types include check valves, which prevent backflow, and butterfly valves, known for their compact design and rapid operation, addressing various defense applications.</p></p>
