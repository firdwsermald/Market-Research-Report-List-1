<p><p>The hydraulic truck market is primarily divided into two types: manual hydraulic and electronic hydraulic trucks. In contrast, electronic hydraulic trucks utilize electric motors for precise control, enhanced efficiency, and reduced physical effort, catering to larger warehouses and logistics operations. Both types serve distinct needs depending on operational requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
