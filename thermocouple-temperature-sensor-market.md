<p><p>The thermocouple temperature sensor market is divided into contact and non-contact types. Contact thermocouple sensors measure temperature through direct physical contact with the object, ensuring accurate readings in various industrial applications. In contrast, non-contact thermocouple sensors utilize infrared technology to gauge temperature from a distance, making them ideal for measuring moving objects or high-temperature processes. Both types are essential in diverse sectors like manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, offering unique advantages based on the specific measurement requirements.</p></p>
