<p><p>Extruded Polystyrene Foam Board (XPS) is available mainly in two types: White EPS and Grey EPS. White EPS, characterized by its lightweight and insulating properties, is commonly used in construction and packaging due to its moisture resistance. Grey EPS, on the other hand, incorporates graphite particles that enhance its thermal performance, making it more efficient at insulation while being slightly denser. Both types serve distinct market needs, with Grey EPS being favored for energy-efficient building applications.</p></p>
