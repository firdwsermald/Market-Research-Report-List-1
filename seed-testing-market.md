<p><p>The seed testing market encompasses various types essential for ensuring seed quality. The weed test identifies the presence of weed seeds, which can impact crop health. The purity test assesses the composition and percentage of desired seed varietals. The viability test determines if seeds are capable of germination, while the germination test measures the actual germination rate under specific conditions. Together, these tests help farmers and producers select high-quality seeds, optimizing yield and reducing potential crop competition.</p></p>
