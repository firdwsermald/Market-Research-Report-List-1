<p><ul><li>Eau de Parfum</li><li>Eau de Toilette</li><li>Eau de Cologne</li><li>Eau Fraiche</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The perfume market comprises several types, each distinguished by concentration and longevity. Eau de Parfum contains a higher concentration of fragrance oils, offering a rich scent that lasts longer, typically 4-8 hours. Eau de Toilette has a lighter concentration, providing a fresh aroma for around 3-5 hours. Eau de Cologne features even less concentration, ideal for refreshing spritzes lasting 2-4 hours. Eau Fraiche contains the lowest concentration, serving as a light, fleeting fragrance option suitable for quick refreshment.</p></p>
