Enhanced chemiluminescence uses advanced reagents and detection mechanisms to amplify light signals, improving sensitivity and specificity in detecting analytes. In contrast, standard chemiluminescence employs basic chemical reactions to generate light without the enhancements, offering a cost-effective solution for routine testing. Both types facilitate rapid, high-throughput diagnostics in laboratories, catering to applications in clinical settings, research, and other fields.</p></p>
