<p><strong>The Pure Steam Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Re-boiler Generator</li><li>Thermosyphon Generator/ External Rising Film Evaporator</li><li>Falling Film Evaporator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pure Steam Generator market includes several types designed for specific applications. The Re-boiler Generator utilizes a boiling chamber to produce pure steam, often used in pharmaceutical industries. The Thermosyphon Generator or External Rising Film Evaporator employs natural circulation for efficient steam generation, favoring continuous operations. Falling Film Evaporators pass liquid downwards over heated surfaces, optimizing heat transfer and steam purity, commonly applied in food processing and chemical sectors. Each type caters to different operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
