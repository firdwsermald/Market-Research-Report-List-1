<p><strong>The Viscosity Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engler Viscosity</li><li>Sagbolt Viscosity</li><li>Redwood Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viscosity tester market encompasses various methods to measure the viscosity of fluids. Engler viscometers evaluate fluids based on their flow time relative to water at a specific temperature. Sagbolt viscometers measure viscosity by timing the descent of a weighted rod through the fluid, providing insights into a fluid's flow characteristics. Redwood viscometers, typically used for petroleum products, determine viscosity by measuring the time it takes for a specific volume of fluid to flow through an orifice. Each method serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
