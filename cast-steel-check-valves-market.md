<p><p>The cast steel check valves market is categorized by different end types, each suited for specific applications. Raised-flange end valves feature a raised rim to create a tighter seal, ideal for high-pressure systems. Ring-type joint end valves utilize a metallic ring to provide a robust seal, ensuring leak-proof connections in critical applications. Butt-weld end valves are designed for seamless connections by welding directly to pipe ends, promoting durability and eliminating potential leak points. Together, these types cater to various industrial needs.</p></p>
