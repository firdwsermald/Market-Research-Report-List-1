<p><p>The Thermal Transfer Films market is primarily divided into two types: Hot Stamping Type and Sublimation Transfer Type. Hot Stamping Type involves applying heat and pressure to transfer a metallic or colored design onto substrates, offering vibrant finishes and durability. In contrast, Sublimation Transfer Type utilizes heat to convert ink into gas, allowing it to penetrate materials like textiles, resulting in high-quality, long-lasting prints. Both types cater to diverse applications, including labels, packaging, and apparel, enhancing visual appeal and branding.</p></p>
