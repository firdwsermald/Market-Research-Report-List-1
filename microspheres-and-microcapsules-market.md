<p><p>Microspheres and microcapsules are advanced materials used in various applications, notably in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food industries. Microspheres are spherical particles typically measuring between 1 to 1000 micrometers, often used for drug delivery, diagnostics, and as contrast agents. They can be made from polymers, ceramics, or metals, offering high surface area, biocompatibility, and controlled release characteristics. Microcapsules, on the other hand, consist of core materials encased in a polymer shell, allowing for the encapsulation of active ingredients such as vitamins or fragrances, enhancing stability and controlled release.</p><p>The Microspheres and Microcapsules Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.6% during the forecast period.
