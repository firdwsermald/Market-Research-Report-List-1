<p><strong>The Pore Pressure Gauges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vented Gauge</li><li>Sealed Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pore pressure gauges are essential in geotechnical and petroleum engineering for measuring fluid pressure within soil pores or rock formations. The vented gauge allows the measurement of pore pressure by referencing the atmosphere, compensating for changes in atmospheric pressure, which enhances accuracy. In contrast, sealed gauges are isolated from external atmospheric pressure, providing reliable readings regardless of environmental changes. Both types cater to different applications, with vented gauges often used in surface measurements and sealed gauges in more challenging subsurface environments.</p></p>
