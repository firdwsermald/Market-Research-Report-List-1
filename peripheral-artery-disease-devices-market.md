<p><p>The Peripheral Artery Disease (PAD) devices market includes various treatment options for managing arterial blockages. Stents are used to support blood vessels and keep them open. Angioplasty balloon catheters widen narrowed arteries by inflating a balloon. Drug-eluting balloons release medication to prevent re-narrowing post-procedure. Atherectomy devices remove plaque from arterial walls. Other devices may include improved imaging technologies and surgical tools. Together, these innovations enhance patient outcomes and address the growing prevalence of PAD globally.</p></p>
