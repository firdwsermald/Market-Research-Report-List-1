<p><ul><li>Holter Monitors</li><li>Resting ECG System</li><li>Stress ECG Monitors</li><li>Event Monitoring Systems</li><li>ECG Management System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECG monitoring systems encompass various types designed for different cardiac assessments. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG recording over 24-48 hours for long-term monitoring of heart rhythm. Resting ECG systems offer immediate analysis by capturing heart activity in a stationary setting. Stress ECG monitors assess heart response during exercise. Event monitoring systems enable patients to record ECG changes during specific episodes. ECG management systems streamline data capture, storage, and analysis, enhancing workflow and improving patient care in clinical environments.</p></p>
