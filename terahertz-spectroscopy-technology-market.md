<p><strong>The Terahertz Spectroscopy Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy</li><li>Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Spectroscopy Technology market encompasses two primary types: Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy (THz-TDS) and Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy (THz-FDS). THz-TDS utilizes pulsed terahertz radiation to obtain time-resolved data, allowing for the analysis of materials with high temporal resolution. In contrast, THz-FDS employs continuous-wave terahertz signals for frequency-dependent measurements, providing detailed spectral information. Both techniques are integral in various applications, including material characterization, biomedical imaging, and security screening, driving advancements in research and industry sectors.</p></p>
