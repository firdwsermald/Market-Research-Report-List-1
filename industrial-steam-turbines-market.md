<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Steam Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reaction Turbines</li><li>Impulse Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial steam turbines market is primarily categorized into two types: reaction turbines and impulse turbines. Reaction turbines operate by converting thermal energy into mechanical energy through both pressure changes and fluid acceleration, utilizing a combination of moving and stationary blades. In contrast, impulse turbines generate power by directing high-pressure steam onto blades, causing them to spin without relying on pressure change. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, influencing efficiency, performance, and energy generation.</p></p>
