<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Scan</li><li>B-Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymetry</li><li>Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic ultrasound system market includes various types tailored for specific diagnostic needs. A-Scan provides axial length measurements essential for cataract surgery planning. B-Scan offers two-dimensional images of the eye, detecting tumors or retinal detachments. Combined Scan merges A-Scan and B-Scan functionalities for comprehensive analysis. Pachymetry measures corneal thickness, crucial for assessing glaucoma risk. Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM) delivers high-resolution images of the anterior segment, aiding in diagnosing complex ocular conditions. Each type plays a vital role in ophthalmic diagnostics.</p></p>
