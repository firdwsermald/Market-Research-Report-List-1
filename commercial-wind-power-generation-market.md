<p><strong>The Commercial Wind Power Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dedicated System Power Generation</li><li>Shared System Power Generation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial wind power generation market consists of two primary types: dedicated system power generation and shared system power generation. Dedicated system power generation involves wind farms that produce energy solely for a specific client or grid, providing a stable and predictable energy supply. In contrast, shared system power generation allows multiple users or stakeholders to access energy produced by a common wind facility, promoting shared investment and risk while benefiting from economies of scale and flexibility in energy distribution.</p></p>
