<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Access Control Fingerprint Module market is primarily divided into two types: capacitive and optical fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electric signals to capture the unique patterns of a fingerprint, providing high accuracy and durability. In contrast, optical fingerprint modules utilize light to scan and capture fingerprint images, often being less costly but potentially less secure. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing security and user authentication in access control systems, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
