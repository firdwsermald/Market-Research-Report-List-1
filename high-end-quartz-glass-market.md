<p><ul><li>Quartz Tube</li><li>Quartz Rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end quartz glass market encompasses various types, primarily quartz tubes and quartz rods. Quartz tubes are hollow cylindrical structures widely used in the semiconductor, lighting, and laboratory industries due to their excellent thermal and chemical resistance. Conversely, quartz rods are solid pieces utilized in applications requiring high purity and strength, such as optical components, furnace tubes, and medical devices. Both products leverage quartz's unique properties, including low thermal expansion and high transmission of ultraviolet light, catering to advanced industrial needs.</p></p>
