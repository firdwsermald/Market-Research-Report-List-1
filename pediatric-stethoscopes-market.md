<p><p>The pediatric stethoscope market comprises primarily two types: dual head and single head stethoscopes. Dual head stethoscopes feature two sides, allowing healthcare professionals to easily switch between diaphragm and bell modes, making them versatile for various body sounds in infants and children. Single head stethoscopes, on the other hand, have one side and are typically lighter and more straightforward, ideal for quick examinations. Both types are designed for comfort and effectiveness, catering specifically to the unique anatomical needs of pediatric patients.</p></p>
