<p><p>Magnetostrictive displacement sensors are categorized based on their ingress protection (IP) ratings, which indicate their resistance to dust and water. IP67 sensors are dust-tight and can withstand immersion in water up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. IP68 sensors offer higher protection, suitable for continuous immersion beyond 1 meter. IP69 sensors are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature cleaning processes. Other sensors may have varying levels of protection, catering to specific applications and environments in industrial settings.</p></p>
