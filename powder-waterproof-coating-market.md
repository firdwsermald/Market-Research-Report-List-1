<p><p>The Powder Waterproof Coating market comprises two main types: liquid waterproof coatings and dry waterproof coatings. Liquid waterproof coatings are typically applied as a fluid, offering versatility for various surfaces and easy application, ideal for intricate or large areas. In contrast, dry waterproof coatings come in a powder form and are activated upon mixing or heating, providing strong durability and resistance in demanding environments. Both types serve critical roles in protecting structures from moisture and enhancing longevity.</p></p>
