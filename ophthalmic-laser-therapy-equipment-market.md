<p><p>Ophthalmic laser therapy equipment encompasses various technologies for treating eye conditions. Femtosecond lasers offer precise tissue cutting for procedures like cataract surgery and LASIK. Excimer lasers are used for corneal reshaping and vision correction. Nd:YAG lasers are effective for treating conditions such as glaucoma and posterior capsule opacification. Diode lasers are utilized for retinal therapies and photocoagulation. Other laser types may include ruby or argon lasers, each serving specific therapeutic purposes in ophthalmology, enhancing treatment outcomes and patient care.</p></p>
